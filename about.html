ABOUT

Tron Ven means "perfectly completed" in Vietnamese. We want to focus on the idea of balance when we come up with the overall concept for the restaurant and it has became our foundation

We want to create a zen dining environment for the clients so they can focus on the tasting experiences.

While the dishes are light and fresh, they have been exquisitely dused with different flavor profiles. This makes each of our dishes an exciting adventure.
